### PRODUCT 1: LumaVue Pocket Projector™ / Movie Night Bundle™
## **DECISION: CONDITIONAL APPROVAL**

**Conditions that MUST be resolved before the first customer order is fulfilled:**

1. **FCC Certification Verification (HARD GATE):** The Product Director flagged FCC compliance as a conditional pass. The Technology Director confirmed this is an Operations gate, not a Tech gate. **Operations Director must obtain and verify the FCC certification number from Vivicine (primary supplier) BEFORE any customer order is shipped.** If Vivicine cannot provide a valid FCC cert number within 72 hours of green light, all LumaVue orders are routed exclusively through CJdropshipping, and CJ's FCC certification must be verified instead. Selling uncertified RF-emitting electronics in the US exposes us to federal enforcement action — this is non-negotiable.

2. **Bundle Routing Decision (HARD GATE):** The Technology Director and Operations Director both flagged that routing Movie Night Bundle orders through DSers/AliExpress results in 3 separate shipments to the customer (projector, screen, case shipped individually). This is unacceptable — it will generate support tickets, confusion, and chargebacks. **My decision: ALL bundle orders route through CJdropshipping US warehouse where items are pre-assembled into a single package.** This adds ~$4.00 to COGS (bundle True COGS rises from $52.79 to ~$56.79, gross margin adjusts from 52.0% to ~48.4%) — still well above our 35% floor. Standalone projector orders may route through Vivicine/DSers. Operations Director: configure this routing before first fulfillment.

3. **Marketing Compliance Review (SOFT GATE):** All landing page copy, ad copy, and social media content for LumaVue must be reviewed by the Technology Director (as the last gate before publishing) to ensure no misleading claims about image quality, resolution, or brightness that could trigger consumer protection issues. The "12,000+ happy customers" claim referenced in the Sales funnel copy **cannot be used at launch** — we have zero customers. Replace with "Join thousands of movie night enthusiasts" or similar non-specific language until we have verifiable customer data. We do not use fake social proof. Ever.

**Reasoning for Conditional Approval:** LumaVue has the highest absolute profit per order ($57.18), strong market timing (R&D confirms we're entering the Early Majority phase with 14-20 months of runway at 80% confidence), and a clear competitive white space (no competitor is executing a content-driven, bundle-first DTC strategy). The bundling strategy is our primary moat. However, the FCC compliance risk and bundle shipping logistics must be locked down before we put a single customer's money at risk. The site can be built and traffic can begin flowing while these conditions are resolved — but no order ships until both hard gates are cleared.

### Why This Sequence (Strategic Logic):

**Principle: Start with the simplest, highest-margin, lowest-risk product to validate the entire operational pipeline before adding complexity.**

This follows the Lean Startup principle of validated learning. ScalpBliss is our minimum viable launch — if our Shopify store, supplier integration, payment processing, email flows, and customer support all work correctly for a simple $34.97 product, we have confidence to handle the more complex LumaVue bundle. If something breaks (DSers misconfiguration, pixel misfiring, Klaviyo flow error), we discover it on a low-COGS product where the financial exposure per failed order is $8.76, not $56.79.

LumaVue follows quickly because its conditions are resolvable in parallel with the ScalpBliss launch. By Day 3-5, we'll have real operational data from ScalpBliss orders flowing through the system, giving us confidence to activate the higher-stakes LumaVue funnel.

AuraGlow comes last not because it's the weakest product, but because the data gap must be closed and because managing three simultaneous launches in Week 1 creates unnecessary operational risk. Staggering by 7-10 days gives each product a clean launch window for content, attention, and debugging.

### Month 1: ZERO Paid Ad Spend Authorized

**Total ad spend approved for Month 1: $0.00**

This is not a cost-cutting measure — it is a strategic decision grounded in first principles:

1. **We have no conversion data.** Running paid ads to an unvalidated funnel is burning cash to learn something we can learn for free with organic traffic. Per the Lean Startup framework, we validate the offer, the funnel, and the conversion rate with organic traffic first. Only then do we know what a customer is worth, which tells us what we can afford to pay to acquire one.

2. **Our organic content strategy is robust.** Marketing has built a comprehensive 7-day content calendar with 3 TikTok posts/day, Instagram Reels repurposing, Pinterest pins, and Facebook Group soft-sell posts. The product categories (projectors, scalp massagers, sunset lamps) are proven viral content categories on TikTok. We give organic a real chance before spending a dollar on ads.

3. **Finance's conservative model shows profitability at 20 orders/month with zero ad spend.** Even the blended conservative scenario (5 LumaVue + 8 ScalpBliss + 7 AuraGlow = 20 total orders) generates $631.55 net profit. We don't need ads to be profitable — we need ads to scale. Scale comes after validation.
