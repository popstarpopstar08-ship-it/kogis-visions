# SECTION 2: PRIORITY RANKING & LAUNCH SEQUENCE

## LAUNCH STRATEGY: SIMULTANEOUS LAUNCH (ALL 3 PRODUCTS WEEK 1)

**Rationale for Simultaneous Launch:**
1. **Risk Diversification:** Three products reduce single-product failure risk. If Laptop Stand underperforms, Gimbal and Blanket provide revenue cushion.
2. **Operational Efficiency:** Single Shopify infrastructure, single supplier integration, single marketing funnel. Launching all three simultaneously is more efficient than staggered launches.
3. **Market Timing:** All three products are at optimal entry windows. Delaying any product risks missing market window (especially Gimbal at +28% YoY growth).
4. **Customer Lifetime Value:** Customers who buy Laptop Stand may also buy Gimbal or Blanket. Cross-selling increases AOV and customer lifetime value.
5. **Organic Traffic Leverage:** TikTok and Reddit audiences overlap (remote workers, productivity enthusiasts, content creators). Single organic campaign can drive traffic to all three products.

**Why NOT Staggered Launch:**
- ❌ Staggered launch delays revenue from viable products
- ❌ Increases operational complexity (three separate launch dates, three separate marketing campaigns)
- ❌ Misses market timing windows (especially Gimbal's +28% growth trajectory)
- ❌ Reduces cross-selling opportunities
